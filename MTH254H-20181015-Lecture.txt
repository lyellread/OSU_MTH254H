Definitions
Domain of a 3D function: The values of X and Y that will make the function output valid output.
Range of a 3D function: The values (z) that a function outputs across its domain.
End_Defenitions

Concepts
Graphing a 3D function: Look at the images of the graph on the planes XZ and YZ - then assemble some graph that includes both.
Solving for Level Curves: Substitute an arbitrary for z, and solve for y and then graph it. Then repeat for another value of z.
End_Concepts
